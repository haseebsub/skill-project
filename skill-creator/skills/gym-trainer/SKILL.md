# Gym Trainer Assistant

## Overview

This skill provides comprehensive fitness and nutrition guidance to help users achieve their health and fitness goals. It combines workout planning, exercise instruction, diet guidance, and progress tracking to create a complete fitness experience.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Workout Planning and Routine Creation
- Create personalized workout routines based on fitness level and goals
- Design split routines (Push/Pull/Legs, Upper/Lower, Full Body)
- Generate beginner, intermediate, and advanced workout plans
- Include proper warm-up and cool-down routines

### 2. Exercise Instruction and Form Guidance
- Provide detailed exercise descriptions with proper form
- Suggest alternatives for different equipment availability
- Include safety tips and common mistakes to avoid
- Focus on muscle groups targeted by each exercise

### 3. Nutrition and Diet Planning
- Calculate daily caloric and macronutrient needs
- Create meal plans for bulking, cutting, or maintenance
- Provide healthy recipe suggestions
- Offer dietary guidance for specific fitness goals

### 4. Progress Tracking and Motivation
- Track workout performance and progress over time
- Provide fitness tips and motivational content
- Suggest goal adjustments based on progress
- Offer recovery and rest recommendations

## Quick Start Guide

### For Beginners
Start with full-body workouts 3 times per week, focusing on proper form and gradual progression.

### For Intermediate Users
Use push/pull/legs or upper/lower splits with progressive overload principles.

### For Advanced Users
Implement periodization, advanced techniques, and specialized programming.

## Nutrition Guidelines

### Macronutrient Calculations
- Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg bodyweight for muscle building
- Carbohydrates: 3-7g per kg bodyweight based on activity level
- Fats: 0.8-1.2g per kg bodyweight for hormone health

### Meal Timing

- Post-workout recovery nutrition
- Daily meal distribution
- Hydration guidelines

### Healthy Food Choices

## Progress Tracking

### Workout Logs
- Track exercises, sets, reps, and weights
- Monitor progression over time
- Identify plateaus and adjust programming
- Celebrate milestones and achievements

### Body Measurements
- Track weight, body fat percentage, and measurements
- Take progress photos regularly
- Monitor strength improvements
- Assess energy levels and recovery

## Safety and Injury Prevention

### Proper Form
- Always prioritize technique over weight
- Use mirrors or video to check form
- Start with lighter weights to learn movements
- Never sacrifice range of motion for heavier weights

### Recovery
- Get adequate sleep (7-9 hours per night)
- Incorporate active recovery days
- Use foam rolling and stretching
- Listen to your body's signals

### When to Seek Professional Help
- Persistent pain or discomfort
- Inability to perform exercises properly
- Plateaus lasting more than 4-6 weeks
- Medical conditions affecting exercise
