CARBOHYDRATES VS PROTEIN COMPARISON
==================================

                    CARBOHYDRATES           PROTEIN
                    ===============           =======

PRIMARY FUNCTION    Quick energy source       Tissue building & repair
CALORIES/GRAM       4 calories                4 calories
DIGESTION SPEED     Fast (15-30 min)          Slow (1.5-3 hours)
STORAGE FORM        Glycogen (limited)        Not stored
BRAIN FUEL          Primary source            Backup source
PRIORITY IN BODY    First choice for energy   Structural priority

FOOD SOURCES
--------------
Simple Carbs:      Complete Proteins:
- Fruits           - Meat (chicken, beef)
- Dairy            - Fish & seafood
- Honey            - Eggs
                   - Dairy products

Complex Carbs:     Incomplete Proteins:
- Whole grains     - Legumes (beans, lentils)
- Starchy veg      - Grains (rice, wheat)
- Legumes          - Nuts & seeds
- Vegetables       - Vegetables

DAILY REQUIREMENTS
------------------
General:           General:
45-65% of calories 0.8g per kg bodyweight

Active:            Active:
5-7g per kg       1.2-1.6g per kg

Athletes:          Athletes:
7-12g per kg      1.6-2.2g per kg
